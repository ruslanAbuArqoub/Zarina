🌿 Apothecary wisdom

Are your ingredients 100% organic?

Yes — every raw ingredient, oil, and botanical we source is either certified organic or wildcrafted from pristine regions. We never use synthetic pesticides or fillers. Each batch is purity‑tested.

How long does shipping take?

Orders are processed within 2‑3 business days. Domestic shipping takes 3‑7 days, international 10‑14 days. You’ll receive a tracking link as soon as your apothecary package leaves our atelier.

Do you offer returns or exchanges?

We accept returns within 14 days for unopened, unused items. For botanical preparations, we offer store credit or a blend exchange – simply contact our herbalists.

Can I customize a blend for my skin type?

Absolutely! Zarina offers a “Mix Your Own Ritual” service. You can select a base oil and herbs, and our apothecary team will create a personalised formula just for you.

Are your products cruelty‑free?

Always. We never test on animals, and every partner supplier is verified cruelty‑free. Our products are 100% vegan except for ethically sourced beeswax in select balms.
