Rooted in nature,
raised by intuition.

Zarina Raw Ingredients was born from a deep reverence for ancient herbalism and the belief that true beauty comes from pure, untouched botanicals. What began as a small kitchen apothecary — blending wildcrafted oils and healing herbs for loved ones — has grown into a sanctuary for those seeking honest, potent skincare.

Each oil, each raw ingredient is mindfully sourced from regenerative farms and ethical wildcrafters. We never compromise on purity: no synthetics, no fillers, only the quiet power of plants. Our name, Zarina, means "golden" — a tribute to the liquid gold of cold‑pressed oils and the luminous ritual of self-care.

Today, our apothecary stands as a gathering place for plant alchemy, slow beauty, and intentional living. We invite you to explore the ingredients that have been cherished for centuries, reimagined for modern rituals.

— with golden intention, Zarina
